# MUGDM.COM — MARKETING AGENT TEAM (Launch Sprint)

> Lean agent structure for shipping mugdm.com in 2-3 days and getting first users. No fluff. Every agent has one job.

---

## The Team (5 Agents)

### Agent 1: LAUNCH COPY WRITER
**Job**: Write all user-facing copy for the app and landing page.
**Runs**: On demand

**Responsibilities:**
- Landing page hero copy (Arabic + English)
- Feature descriptions for 4 core modules (Compliance, Vault, Bookkeeper, Calendar)
- Onboarding screen copy (5 steps)
- Email templates: welcome email, CR expiry reminder, compliance deadline alert
- App empty states ("No documents yet — upload your CR to get started")
- Error messages and success confirmations
- Meta descriptions and OG tags for SEO

**Voice:** Professional but warm. Like a trusted advisor, not a bank. Arabic: MSA with Saudi business terms. English: Clear, direct, no jargon. Always action-oriented.

**Output format**: JSON files matching the i18n structure (ar.json / en.json namespaces)

---

### Agent 2: SOCIAL MEDIA LAUNCHER
**Job**: Create 2 weeks of launch content for Twitter/X and LinkedIn.
**Runs**: On demand, then scheduled weekly

**Week 1 (Pre-launch):** Teaser posts, problem statements, behind-the-scenes, feature previews, launch announcement
**Week 2 (Post-launch):** Learnings, testimonials, feature spotlights, community engagement, demo videos

**Hashtags:** #mugdm #سجل_تجاري #ريادة_أعمال #compliance #Saudi_startups

---

### Agent 3: SEO & LANDING PAGE OPTIMIZER
**Job**: Make mugdm.com rank for Saudi entrepreneur searches.
**Runs**: Once before launch, then monthly

**Target keywords:**
- High: سجل تجاري تجديد, CR renewal Saudi, محاسبة مشاريع صغيرة
- Medium: محفظة وثائق, deadline tracker business
- Low: برنامج محاسبة سحابي, cloud accounting software

**Landing page structure:** Hero (problem > solution > CTA) > Pain point > 4 feature cards > How it works (3 steps) > Security section > CTA > FAQ

---

### Agent 4: OUTREACH & COMMUNITY BUILDER
**Job**: Get the first 50 users through direct outreach.
**Runs**: Daily during launch week

**Channels:** Saudi Twitter/X, LinkedIn, WhatsApp entrepreneur groups, accountant/consultant partnerships, Monsha'at forums

**Key tactic:** Accountant partnerships. One accountant with 30 SME clients recommending mugdm = months of social media effort. Target Saudi Auditors Association and SOCPA-certified accountants.

---

### Agent 5: ANALYTICS & FEEDBACK COLLECTOR
**Job**: Track what's working from day one.
**Runs**: Scheduled daily after launch

**Week 1 targets:** 50+ signups, >60% onboarding completion, >30% CR upload rate, >25% Day 2 return visits

**Feedback collection:** In-app prompt after 3rd login, post-onboarding survey, weekly email to early users

## 3-Day Launch Timeline

### Day 1: BUILD
- Fix auth (login/signup/logout working)
- Onboarding flow (CR upload + profile)
- Deploy to Vercel + connect mugdm.com domain
- Write landing page copy + onboarding copy
- Optimize meta tags, OG image

### Day 2: POLISH & PREP
- Dashboard with real data from Supabase
- Document Vault (upload + list)
- Compliance Calendar (display deadlines)
- Write email templates
- Queue pre-launch social posts
- Set up analytics

### Day 3: LAUNCH
- Final testing on mobile (Arabic + English)
- Publish launch announcement
- Start direct outreach
- Monitor signups and feedback
- Fix critical bugs

## Distribution Playbook (Saudi-Specific)

### Top Channels (Ranked by ROI for solo founder):
1. **Direct DMs** to entrepreneurs on Twitter/X — highest conversion, lowest effort
2. **Accountant partnerships** — one accountant = 30 potential users
3. **WhatsApp groups** — Saudi business runs on WhatsApp
4. **Monsha'at** — government SME authority, 40,000+ businesses in ecosystem
5. **LinkedIn** — founder story posts perform well in Saudi professional circles
6. **Arabic SEO** — competitors neglect this, easy wins available

### Referral Mechanic:
"Invite a business owner, both get priority access to new features"
Simple, no cash incentives needed at this stage.
