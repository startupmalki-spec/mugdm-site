# IFRS Adoption in KSA (SOCPA)

SOCPA has endorsed International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) as
the basis for financial reporting in Saudi Arabia, with limited local
amendments and additional disclosures.

Publicly summarised:

- **Listed entities and publicly accountable entities:** required to apply
  "IFRS as endorsed in KSA" (full IFRS plus SOCPA amendments).
- **Other entities (SMEs):** may apply "IFRS for SMEs as endorsed in KSA",
  subject to the eligibility criteria published by SOCPA.
- Financial statements must follow SOCPA's additional disclosure
  requirements, including zakat and tax-related notes where relevant.

Transition dates, endorsement notes and any local amendments are
maintained by SOCPA and updated periodically.

Source: https://socpa.org.sa/Socpa/Technical-Resources/Accounting-standards.aspx
