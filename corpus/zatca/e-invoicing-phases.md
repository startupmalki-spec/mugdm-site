# E-Invoicing (Fatoora) Phases

ZATCA's e-invoicing programme, "Fatoora", rolls out in two public phases:

- **Phase 1 — Generation (live since 4 December 2021):** all VAT-registered
  residents must issue and store tax invoices and related notes in a
  structured electronic format. Handwritten or plain-image invoices are
  not compliant.
- **Phase 2 — Integration (started 1 January 2023, waved by turnover):**
  taxpayers must integrate their e-invoicing solution with ZATCA's Fatoora
  platform, generate invoices in the required XML/PDF-A3 format, include
  a cryptographic stamp and UUID, and transmit B2B invoices for clearance
  before issuance. B2C (simplified) invoices are reported within 24 hours.

Each Phase 2 wave covers taxpayers above a specific annual revenue
threshold and is announced by ZATCA at least six months before the wave's
go-live date.

Source: https://zatca.gov.sa/en/E-Invoicing/Pages/default.aspx
