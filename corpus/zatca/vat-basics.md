# VAT Basics (KSA)

The standard Value Added Tax (VAT) rate in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is
**15%**, applicable to most goods and services supplied by taxable persons.

VAT registration thresholds (publicly summarised):

- **Mandatory registration:** annual taxable supplies exceeding SAR 375,000.
- **Optional registration:** annual taxable supplies between SAR 187,500
  and SAR 375,000.
- Businesses below SAR 187,500 are generally not eligible to register.

VAT return filing frequency depends on annual taxable revenue:

- **Monthly filing** is required when annual taxable revenue exceeds
  **SAR 40 million**.
- **Quarterly filing** applies below that threshold.

Returns and payments are submitted through the ZATCA portal. Late filing or
payment can trigger penalties; consult the official guidance for current
rates.

Source: https://zatca.gov.sa/en/RulesRegulations/Taxes/Pages/VAT.aspx
