# GOSI Registration Obligations

Every private-sector employer in Saudi Arabia must register the
establishment with GOSI and enrol eligible employees. Public summary:

- Employers must register the establishment with GOSI upon commencement
  of activity and before hiring the first employee.
- Each new employee must be enrolled with GOSI from their first working
  day; failing to enrol on time can trigger back-contributions and fines.
- Changes to salary, job role, or end-of-service must be reported through
  the GOSI online portal.
- Monthly contribution files are submitted and paid via the GOSI portal.

Source: https://www.gosi.gov.sa/GOSIOnline/Employers_Services
