# GOSI Contribution Rates (Summary)

GOSI contributions in the private sector are split between employer and
employee. Publicly summarised rates:

- **Saudi employees:**
  - Employer: ~12% (9% Annuities + 2% Occupational Hazards + 1% Unemployment/SANED)
  - Employee: ~10% (9% Annuities + 1% Unemployment/SANED)
- **Non-Saudi employees:**
  - Employer only: 2% Occupational Hazards contribution.

Contributions are calculated on the contributory wage, subject to GOSI's
published floor and ceiling. Monthly contributions are due within the
statutory window following the end of each wage month; late payment incurs
penalties defined by GOSI.

Rates and thresholds are periodically updated — always confirm against the
official GOSI rate schedule.

Source: https://www.gosi.gov.sa/GOSIOnline/Contributions_Rates
